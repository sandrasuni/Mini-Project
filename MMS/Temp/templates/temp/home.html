About Us

Welcome to Chaithram caters

At the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC), hostels and mess facilities are designed to offer students a home away from home. Located amidst the scenic beauty of Calicut, our residential and dining facilities are integral to the campus life, providing students with a nurturing environment to live, learn, and grow

The MLH Mess at NIT Calicut is dedicated to providing students with nutritious and varied meals in a hygienic and welcoming environment. Attached to the MLH Hostel, the mess offers a balanced menu catering to both vegetarian and non-vegetarian preferences. With a focus on quality and cleanliness, the MLH Mess ensures that students enjoy wholesome food in a community-oriented setting, making it a vital part of their campus life.
